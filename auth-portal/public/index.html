Password Reset Help

When should I reset my password?

Reset your password if you've forgotten it, suspect unauthorized access, or want to update it for security reasons.

How do I make sure I can reset my password?

To enable password resets, you must associate an email address with your account:

  • When creating your account, provide an email address
  • After creating an account, add an email in Settings â†’ Email options
I have multiple accounts with the same email

If you enter only your email address, you'll receive separate reset emails for all accounts associated with that address.

To reset just one account, enter only the username.

I'm getting password reset emails I didn't request

Enable Enhanced Password Reset (EPR) in Settings. This requires both username AND email to trigger a reset, preventing unwanted emails from people who know just one piece of information.

I didn't receive a reset email

Possible reasons:

  • No email address on your account
  • Misspelled username or email
  • EPR is enabled but you only entered one field
  • Email went to spam/junk folder
  • Too many reset requests (wait and try again)
